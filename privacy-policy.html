Our Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

At Richclemon Language School, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from you in various ways, including when you register on our website, subscribe to our newsletter, or enroll in our courses. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Mailing Address
  • Payment Information

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect may be used in the following ways:

  • To process course registrations and payments
  • To send you updates about our courses, promotions, or services
  • To improve our website and services
  • To respond to your inquiries and customer service requests

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties, except when required by law or necessary to fulfill a service you have requested (such as processing payments).

4. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Choices

You have the right to opt out of receiving our marketing emails by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in those emails. You can also request to update, access, or delete your personal information by contacting us at privacy@richclemon.com.

6. Use of Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience and gather information about site traffic. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may limit the functionality of our site.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Richclemon reserves the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the latest update will be noted at the bottom of the policy. Your continued use of our services after any changes indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@richclemon.com.
